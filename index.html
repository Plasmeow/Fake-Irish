What's with the name?

The purpose of this blog is to bring forth a united Ireland by poking fun at the concept of being "fake Irish". While this term may have a different definition in online spaces, my personal experience with it is completely separate. These words have been used against me by people of the Republic of Ireland to say I'm "not Irish enough" to call myself Irish, despite being from Northern Ireland. Which, you know, has the word Ireland in it, so... it would make me Irish (please do not look further into this logic).

Of course, we have our differences. We don't use the same currency, for one, but we are both very Christian. I'm just not sure who "those damn protestants" are anymore, was it you or me?
